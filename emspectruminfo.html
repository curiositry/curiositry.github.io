Emspectrum.info

Mon 03 February 2014


A Visual Tour of the Electromagnetic Spectrum

Contribute to Repo

The Electromagnetic Spectrum pervades everything. The sounds in your ears; the words you speak; the cellphone you love to distraction; the radio and TV that blare in the background — even the light from this screen hitting your eyes. Do you understand it?

emspectrum.info will allow you to discover the wonders of the ubiquitous electromagnetic spectrum.

Technology

At the moment the SVGs are being generated with a python script and the data is stored in a MySQL DB. Emspectrum.info in under develoment, and we will be opening for signups shortly. The GitHub repo is currently empty, but drop and line if you’re interested in contributing.

Proudly powered by Pelican, which takes great advantage of Python.
