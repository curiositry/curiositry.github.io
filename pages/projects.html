About

This site is a place to collect my web projects, and also was a way for me to to familiarize myself to some extent with Pelican.

This site is generated with Pelican, a Python static site generator. It makes use of Typeplate.css, Pygments.css, Reset.css, Python, Jinja2 templating, and my custom Pelican theme, Clean.
